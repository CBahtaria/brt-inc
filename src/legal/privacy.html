Privacy Policy

This policy covers how BRT Inc. collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Effective: 2 July 2026. Last updated: 2 July 2026.

1. What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to respond to your enquiry and operate this website.

Data Purpose Where it lives Retention
Name, email address, message submitted via the contact form Replied to Google Workspace email Duration of client relationship + 3 years, then deleted
IP address (automatic, from your browser) Infrastructure security and abuse prevention Vercel edge network logs Vercel's own retention policy (up to 1 year)
Cookies / analytics / third-party embeds None. We set no cookies, run no analytics, and embed no third-party content.

2. Who has access

BRT Inc. personnel only. We use two infrastructure sub-processors:

  • Vercel (SOC 2 Type II certified) — hosts this website and processes server logs. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google Workspace — stores email correspondence. Google Workspace DPA

We do not sell data, share with data brokers, or use your information for AI training.

3. Your rights

You have the right to access, delete, or correct any personal data we hold about you. These rights are available to everyone regardless of jurisdiction. To exercise them, email privacy@brtinc.dev and we will respond within 30 days.

4. Security measures

  • Google Workspace MFA enforced on all accounts
  • Vercel SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
  • HTTPS with TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Rate limiting on the contact form to prevent abuse
  • Annual internal security review

5. Breach notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we commit to notifying you within 72 hours of becoming aware of it — consistent with GDPR Article 34 obligations.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@brtinc.dev.
