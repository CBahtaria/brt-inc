§1 Services & Scope

BRT Inc. (operated by Charles Bartaria, "we", "us") provides freelance software engineering services including web application development, security auditing, data science, AI/ML modelling, DevOps infrastructure, and maintenance retainers.

1.1 Proposals

All fixed-scope work begins with a written proposal specifying deliverables, price (in ZAR), and timeline. The proposal is valid for 14 days from issue date. Payment of a deposit constitutes acceptance of the proposal and these Terms.

1.2 Scope changes

Any change to agreed deliverables requires a written amendment. Work outside the original scope is quoted separately and requires additional deposit before commencement.

1.3 Client obligations

The Client must provide timely access to systems, credentials, and feedback required to complete the work. Delays caused by late Client responses may extend the agreed timeline proportionally at no additional charge.

§2 Payment Terms (ZAR)

All prices are quoted and invoiced in South African Rand (ZAR) unless otherwise agreed. USD or other currencies may be accepted at the exchange rate on the invoice date.

2.1 Deposit

Fixed-scope projects require a deposit (typically 50%) before work begins. Hourly engagements are invoiced weekly with payment due within 7 days of invoice.

2.2 Final payment

The balance is due on delivery of the agreed deliverables. Final code and documentation are released upon receipt of full payment.

2.3 Late payment

Invoices unpaid after 7 days accrue interest at 2% per month. BRT Inc. reserves the right to suspend work on active retainers if invoices remain unpaid after 14 days.

2.4 Payment methods

Accepted: Wise (USD/ZAR), PayPal, South African EFT, or bank transfer. Payment details are provided on the invoice.

2.5 Refunds

Deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced. If work has not commenced within 14 days of deposit payment and the Client requests cancellation, the deposit is refunded in full.

Depositing constitutes agreement to these Terms. Please read them before paying.
