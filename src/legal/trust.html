Trust & Security

We expect our clients to vet us. Here's what we've got — ask for documents during diligence.

Last updated: 2 July 2026.

Cyber Liability Insurance

We carry cyber liability E&O insurance covering technology errors & omissions, data breach response, and third-party liability. Coverage details and a certificate of insurance are available during vendor diligence on request.

Internal Security Practices

  • All devices managed with MDM and full-disk encryption
  • Password manager required — no shared passwords
  • MFA enforced on email, hosting, repositories, and client systems
  • Client data segmented per engagement — no commingling
  • Annual internal security review

Background Checks

All personnel who access client systems undergo:

  • Criminal background check (county and federal level)
  • Employment verification
  • Professional reference checks

Data Processing Agreement

A standard DPA is available covering:

  • Subject matter, purpose, and duration
  • Obligations and rights of the controller
  • Sub-processor disclosure
  • Retention and deletion commitments
  • Breach notification (24-hour internal commitment)
  • Cross-border transfer safeguards

BAA available for HIPAA-covered engagements. We understand the difference. Open to reasonable redlines.

What We Don't Have (honest)

  • No SOC 2 attestation for BRT Inc. itself. SOC 2 costs R555,000–R1,480,000/year. We've chosen technical controls and insurance instead. If SOC 2 is a hard requirement, we'll refer you to firms that have it.
  • No 24/7 SOC. For continuous monitoring needs, we partner with MDR providers and can help you select one.

Vendor diligence package (insurance certificate, sample redacted reports, references) available by email — no NDA required. hello@brtinc.dev
